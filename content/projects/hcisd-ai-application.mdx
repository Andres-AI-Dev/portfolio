Currently an internal AI-powered application designed to help improve efficiency for administrators across Harlingen CISD, which serves over 16,000 students across more than 30 campuses.

## Overview

The HCISD AI Application is a district-wide platform that leverages cutting-edge AI technology to provide personalized learning experiences, administrative insights, and intelligent educational support.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React + Vite
- **Backend**: Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Functions)
- **AI Engine**: Google Vertex AI with Genkit
- **Additional Services**: Google Workspace Integration

## Key Features

### For Students
- Personalized AI tutoring and learning assistance
- Instant feedback on assignments
- Adaptive learning pathways

### For Teachers
- AI-powered lesson planning assistance
- Automated grading support
- Student progress analytics

### For Administrators
- District-wide analytics dashboard
- Resource allocation insights
- Policy and guideline integration

## Implementation Highlights

- **TEKS Integration**: Aligned with Texas Essential Knowledge and Skills standards
- **RAG with Weaviate**: Custom knowledge base incorporating district policies and internal documentation
- **Multi-Agent Orchestration**: Role-based chat flows for different user types
- **Google Workspace Integration**: Seamless connection with Google Drive and Sites

## Impact

Working with Dr. Jessica Hruska (HCISD Director of Innovative Practices) and external partners including Google's education team, this application is pioneering AI implementation in K-12 education in Texas.

## Current Status

Active development and deployment across HCISD campuses, with ongoing collaboration with Region 16 ESC and Google for Education.
